  • TVET

    TVET under UPKB offers practical, skills-based training that aligns with industry needs. This program enables students to acquire specialized skills, allowing them to enter the workforce directly or pursue higher-level studies.

    Learn More

  • UiTM

    UiTM Diploma provides education aligned with industry needs and broad career opportunities. It emphasizes theory, practical skills, and character development to produce competitive graduates.

    Learn More

  • UM

    The UM Diploma provides high-quality education built on research and comprehensive learning. This program equips students with academic and professional skills to produce versatile graduates.

    Learn More

  • UTM

    The UTM Diploma is a study program that focuses on technology, engineering, and innovation. It produces competent students with a strong technical foundation and broad career pathway opportunities.

    Learn More

  • UIC

    UIC is a private college that offers diploma programs in a conducive learning environment with complete facilities. Students can pursue UIC’s own programs or collaborative programs with reputable universities, allowing them to obtain recognized qualifications.
